Clic

Clic offers a smooth and powerful experience when presenting customers with all customisation options for their hi-fi furniture. The program guides users into visualising their ideas and helps them make the right purchase decision.

Live Link ↗

Role & responsibilities

  • Synthesized findings into visual canvases to guide design decisions and developed user flows

  • Designed key interface screens, focusing on accessibility and ease of use and conducted usability testing

  • Created onboarding and visualisation models to match interactive features

  • Analysed feedback and implemented iterative improvements

The challenge



Customers often struggle to visualise how furniture will look in their homes, leading to uncertainty in the buying process. Additionally, dealers face difficulties in effectively communicating the wide range of customisation options available, resulting in long decision-making times and potential dissatisfaction. The challenge is to create a more comfortable decision making and buying process for clients, while also supporting the salespersons who meets them in-store.



The problem




How can Clic offer the customer a way to experience their products and tailor individual solutions?
